Recipe #1

Fried eggs

  • Time: 15 minute
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 2
Oil 100ml

Step 1: Break the egg

Step 2: Fill pan with the oil

Step 3: Fry on high heat

Step 4: After frying, serve the fried eggs on plate and add pepper
